Early access members get 30 days free with full access to every feature. No credit card required to join the waitlist.
Do I need investments in both India and the US?
Not at all. Dhan-X works equally well if you only have Indian investments, only US accounts, or a mix of both. Use the features that are relevant to you.

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. AI features strip personal identifiers before any processing — your PAN, name, and account numbers never leave our secure servers.
