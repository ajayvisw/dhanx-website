Your AI coach is ready
for any decision
From big life decisions to small portfolio tweaks — Dhan-X gives you the numbers and the context to decide with confidence.
"Should I buy a house now or keep renting?"
Dhan-X runs the full rent vs buy analysis with your actual rent, home price, and investment return — and tells you exactly when buying makes sense.
"Am I paying too much in mutual fund fees?"
Dhan-X shows your TER leakage — how much regular plans cost vs direct — and calculates exactly how much you'd save by switching.
"What's my actual net worth across all accounts?"
Connect your India MFs, US brokerage, and add real estate and gold. Dhan-X gives you one number — in rupees or dollars.

The FI calculator takes your monthly expenses, expected returns, and inflation — and tells you your number and how many years away you are.
"Which stocks should I be watching?"
Ask in plain English. The AI screener finds stocks matching your criteria — with live prices, fundamentals, and a 30-day chart.
"Am I spending too much this month?"
The budget planner tracks income vs expenses and shows your savings rate, guilt-free spending, and how much you can invest.
