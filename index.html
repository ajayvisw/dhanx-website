From big life decisions to small portfolio tweaks — Dhan-X gives you the numbers and the context to decide with confidence.
"Should I buy a house now or keep renting?"
Dhan-X runs the full rent vs buy analysis with your actual rent, home price, and investment return — and tells you exactly when buying makes sense.
"Am I paying too much in mutual fund fees?"
Dhan-X shows your TER leakage — how much regular plans cost vs direct — and calculates exactly how much you'd save by switching.
