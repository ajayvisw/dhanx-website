Our story

Built by an investor
for investors like us

Dhan-X was born from a simple frustration — no single tool understood the complexity of having investments in India and the US at the same time.

THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

Your wealth doesn't fit in one spreadsheet

If you have Indian mutual funds, US brokerage accounts, real estate, and gold — you know the pain. No tool gives you a complete picture. You end up with 5 different apps, manual spreadsheets, and no idea what your actual XIRR is.

Dhan-X changes that. We built a platform that understands the specific complexity of cross-border Indian investors — from CAMS and NSDL integration to Schwab and Vanguard connectivity, from TER leakage analysis to rent vs buy in the Indian context.

OUR JOURNEY

2024 — The idea

Spent 15 years in the US building wealth across multiple accounts. Moved back to India and realised no tool could show the complete picture.

2025 — Building

Relocated to Hyderabad. Started building Dhan-X with a focus on XIRR accuracy, CAMS/NSDL integration, and AI-powered insights.

2026 — Private beta

Launched private beta with a small group of HNI investors. Iterating based on real feedback from cross-border investors.

Coming soon — Public launch

Opening Dhan-X to all investors with cross-border portfolios. Join the waitlist to be first.
